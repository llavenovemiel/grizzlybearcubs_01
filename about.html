Meet the Grizzly Cubs Team

ABOUT US

Grizzly Cubs Den Children’s Activities and Recreation is the newest indoor playground in Edmonton for the whole family that offers many activities and classes for children that will help them develop their well being and start building friendship and good relationship with their family members at a young age.

Grizzly Cubs Den is valuable for strengthening many areas of development and learning. Our company aims to support young children’s learning of social-emotional skills, general cognitive development and physical strength. Grizzly Cubs Den focuses on long term goal of encouraging kids play sports and learn music at a young age; we intend to develop confidence and a positive self-image in each child using general motor imitation skills, over-all fitness, strength, speed & agility, flexibility and cardiovascular fitness. With a variety of engaging activity options, every child will benefit from the programs and activities that encourage creativity, imagination, language, literacy, confidence, cultures, fun and independence. Grizzly Cubs Den’s Family Indoor playground brings family together in fun. Every child will grow happy and healthy.

Our indoor playground also offers a whole lot of fun experience for any age group including parents, grandparents, teenagers, group of professional or for people who are seeking to make friends and have fun! We also have a great cafeteria to keep you content and happy.
